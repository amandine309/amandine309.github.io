How I work

A mix of precision, curiosity and iteration.

01

I build to understand.

I learn best by turning theory into something tangible: a working prototype, a system, a visual, a tool, or a full project from scratch.

02

I like hard debugging.

I enjoy problems where the answer is not obvious at first: tracing behaviour, testing hypotheses, and slowly making the system clearer.

03

I care about the finish.

For me, good work is not only functional. It should be readable, intentional, well-presented, and pleasant to use or explore.

Path

The thread through my projects.

02

Turning courses into real projects

What I bring
